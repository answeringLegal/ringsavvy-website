11 Of The Best Tools For Landscapers

Joe GalottiNovember 30th, 2020

If you’re ready to make a few upgrades to your collection of tools or are looking for a great functional gift for a professional landscaper in your life, you’ve come to the right place. In this post, we’ll introduce you to 11 of the top tools used by landscapers today. The items on this list all come from top tool manufacturers and can be found on Amazon. Best Lawn Mower

Ariens 915223 IKON-X 52″ Zero Turn Mower

Yes it’s true, you’re going to have to do quite a few jobs to cover the cost of this mower. But, if you’re mowing multiple lawns on an almost daily basis, do you really want to invest in a cheap device that might break down after a few months? Instead, make a smart investment for your business, by picking up this strong and sturdy mower from Ariens that is likely to get you through several summers. This powerful machine comes with 23 horsepower energy to the mower. According to an article from Jens Jensen, you expect this product to wipe out about an acre of mowing in just 20 minutes. Also worth mentioning is the mower’s hydrostatic drive system (which will require almost no maintenance), adaptable cutting deck size and padded back seat.

Best Leaf Blower

TORO 51624 PowerJet F700

This product from the Toro garden company was selected by Wirecutter as their top leaf blower pick of 2020. In their evaluation of the item, they said it was the most powerful electric blower they’ve found in six years of testing, and described it as like an air bazooka. When a New York Times website is using the term “air bazooka”, you know it must be an impressive tool. This blower features up to 725 CFM and reaches an air speed of up to 140 MPH, which should be more than enough volume to help you move piles of heavy and wet leaves.

Best Snow Blower

Husqvarna ST224 24-Inch Two Stage Electric Start Snow Blower

No, this isn’t the cheapest of snow blowers. But when you need to take care of heavy, wet snow in a hurry, this Husqvarna model will always be up for the task. The blower is equipped with 15-inch tires, a steel ribbon auger, power steering and adjustable skid shoes, ensuring that snow removal workers can always keep moving when they have to clear large driveaways or long paths. Also present on the device is a remote chute deflector, which allows users to make quick adjustments on the fly while cutting through snow, and do without having to step out from behind the machine.

Best Digging Shovel

Fiskars Long-Handled Digging Shovel

It’s not uncommon to hear landscapers complain about back and knee pain. This is of course because so much of their work involves awkward bending of the body. While soreness can’t be completely eliminated from the landscaper’s day, using the right shovel can help significantly decrease it. That’s why all yard workers will want to invest in this product from Fiskars, which makes it easy to dig through tough soil without constantly bending. The item pairs a lengthy handle with an all-steel design, allowing it to be a highly efficient and durable tool. Landscapers will also love the extra-large foot platform, which can be used to help maximize force when driving the blade into soil.

Purchase Here

Best Snow Shovel

FMRC Seymour Commercial Snow Shovel

When investing in a shovel for snow removal jobs, the first box you need to check is durability. You really don’t want to be buying another shovel halfway through the winter. Thankfully this model from Seymour has been built to thrive in heavy-wear situations. The blade of the shovel is 18-inches wide and is made of extremely strong ABS material. This is a blade that can be relied upon to stay intact, even when constantly scraping against a concrete floor. The shovel is also notable for its handle, which is 44 inches long and constructed with tough fiberglass material that won’t break under the pressure of heavy snow. Workers will love how light this item feels in their hands, and how easy it is to maneuver and carry around with them throughout the day.

Best Chainsaw

Poulan Pro 20 in. 50cc 2-Cycle Gas Chainsaw

Usually when selecting a chainsaw you’re forced to choose between a more powerful engine or a cleaner running engine. This product from Poulan Pro helps you enjoy the best of both worlds, as it has been built with OxyPower engine technology, which offers plenty of power, but also 70% less emissions and 20% lower fuel consumption. This is truly the chainsaw of the future, as it features a user friendly combined choke/stop control, which allows for faster starting and decreased risk of engine flooding. Additionally, the filters of the items are easy to access, making maintenance incredibly simple.

Best Pruning Shears

Corona BP 6250 MAXForged Aluminum Bypass Hand Pruner

Get ready to enjoy clean cut after clean cut once you start using this pruning device from Corona Tools. The item has been aluminum forged and been given high-carbon steel blades, giving it superior strength and sharpness. It also has lightweight aluminum handles, allowing landscapers to get trimming tasks done with little resistance. The product is designed to take on the thickest of limbs and branches, and make close cuts which will help plants heal faster. The shock-absorbing bumper of the pruner also gives users a more comfortable cutting experience.

Best Weed Wacker

Husqvarna 324L 4-Cycle 18″ Cutting Path Gas String Trimmer

Today’s weed eaters are built to be such impressive looking machines, that they often come off as a bit daunting to use. This model from Husqvarna couldn’t be easier to handle, as the weight of the item is extremely balanced. It also has a smart starting system that allows landscapers to always get it going on the first pull. Users will also find that the device offers professional grade power, and has a four-cycle engine, which runs on unleaded gas and reduces emissions. The product features an 18-inch cutting width, and lawn workers will be amazed by just how smoothly this trimmer cuts.

Best Steel Rake

RazorBack 2.13″ X 24″ X 68″ Fiberglass Handle Steel Rake Wit

During the final four months of the year, most landscapers will find themselves constantly wrangling leaves, stones and twigs. In order to be successful during that period of time, having a highly durable rake is absolutely paramount. This model from RazorBack is about as sturdy as they come. The rake’s 24 steel head twines will never pull loose. Its 54.5-inch fiberglass handle is stronger than typical wood handles, making it less likely to break down over time. The product is also incredibly easy to use, as it comes with a 9-inch cushioned grip and has a spring mechanism in the center of the rake head that controls recoil for smooth pulling action.

Best Wheelbarrow

Gorilla Carts GOR866D Heavy-Duty Garden Poly Dump Cart

With a 1,200-pound carrying capacity, this wheelbarrow from Gorilla Carts is ready to handle anything landscapers might throw at it. The item features a top-notch steel frame, a tub constructed with tough polyethylene material and 13-inch robust tires. It’s truly one of the toughest carts on the market today, and it’s built out of rust free material to ensure it doesn’t wear down easily over time. It also comes with a gripped and adjustable prolonged handle, which allows for easy pulling, and navigating through rocky fields. Also notable is the product’s patented quick-release dump design, which saves time and reduces user fatigue.

Best Yard Waste Bag

WORX Landscaping Collapsible Yard Waste Bag

We’ll round out our list with a highly affordable and useful item that landscapers would be wise to purchase a few of. This collapsible bin from WORX Tools is ideal for cleaning up large yards and fields in the fall, as it can contain up to 26 gallons of waste, is 18 1/2-inches in diameter and 22-inches tall. While the bag is fairly lightweight and easy to move, it’s built surprisingly strong, and is far less likely to rip than a typical yard bag. The handles on each side of the bag make carrying it around and emptying it an effortless task. The product also collapses into a slim 3-inch stack, making it convenient to store.

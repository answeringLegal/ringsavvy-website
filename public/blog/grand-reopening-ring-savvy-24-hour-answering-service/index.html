Grand Reopening: Ring Savvy’s 24 Hour Answering Service is Back

Nick WerkerNovember 30th, 2020

We’ve got exciting news for all small business owners as we enter the year 2020: Ring Savvy is now open and ready for new business!

You’re probably thinking,

“Why should I care?”

“Oh great, another answering service that over-promises and under-delivers.”

Well, that couldn’t be further from the truth. For years, Ring Savvy has been providing the absolute best in 24 hour answering services, by employing receptionists who live in our neighborhood, never outsourcing any of our calls, speaking both English and Spanish at all hours of the day and night, performing customizable new customer intakes, dispatching calls to your reps/drivers, and much more. We just haven’t taken on any new customers in a long time.

Let’s talk more about what Ring Savvy offers that’s better than the competition.

24 Hour Answering Service: Ring Savvy answers every call that you can’t get to with a live receptionist, who is trained to answer for your specific business. When you become a customer of Ring Savvy, we help you set up and customize every service that we have to offer, and we then train our staff to recognize and follow the directions on your account. Having a 24 hour answering service means that none of your callers, whether they be new or existing customers, will ever have to deal with your pesky voicemail or long hold times ever again. This means happier customers, and more new business for you.

Intelligent Dispatching: For our plumbing, HVAC, contracting, or any other service with techs out in the field, we’ve got our intelligent dispatching service. This means that, according to your schedule, and depending on the type of caller to your business, our virtual receptionist will call the right person on your team and send them to the job site. We’ll let your caller know who is on the way and when they can be expected, so that you get the job done quickly and your caller gets their problem fixed. You set your schedule, we just follow it.

Appointment Scheduling: Does your business require you to keep a schedule of who you have to meet with, when, and what you’ll need to know heading into that conversation? Well, Ring Savvy has you covered. Our agents can schedule appointments, either on the phone or in person, and provide you with the details of the who, what, when, and where. We won’t double book you, and you can determine your availability – we just book your appointments according to your pre-determined schedule.

New Customer Intake: Is the person calling you a potential new customer, and do they want some information about what you do and what you can offer? Great! We’ll answer frequently asked questions about your business, and then we’ll make sure to ask the caller your qualifying questions. We’ll help you customize a new customer intake script that our virtual receptionists will follow that will help you maximize your chance of converting that caller into a paying customer.

So, what does the Grand Reopening of Ring Savvy’s 24 hour answering service mean for your business?
